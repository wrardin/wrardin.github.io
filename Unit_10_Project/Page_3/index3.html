Rum Runners Island Style

Ingredient Name Amount Picture of Ingredient
Dark Rum 1oz
Light Rum 1oz
Dark Spiced Rum 1oz
Banana Liqueur 1/2 oz
Blackberry Brandy 1/2 oz
Genadine splash
Sweetend Lime Juice splash
151 Rum (optional) floated splash
Hawaiian Punch 8 oz

Mixing Instructions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well
  3. Strain into a hurricane glass over fresh ice
  4. Garnish with fruit and enjoy

⇶Make this rum runner a frozen drink by pouring the ingredients into a blender with about 3/4 cup of ice. Blend until smooth.
