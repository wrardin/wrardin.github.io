One of Williams Favorite Book Series

The Universe of Dune

Started in 1965, this series is set almost 20,000 years in the future. Tells the story of Paul Atreides and his rise to power. Written by the late Frank Herbert, his use of religious and political imagery is very crafty in its use. He then went on to expand on this over the next 20 years and six books.

Then over a decade later Frank's son Brian Herbert and fellow writer Kevin J. Anderson with the use of Frank's notes began to expand upon the Dune Universe with more stories giving us more history and greater background into the characters.
